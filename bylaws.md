# 1 Identity
## 1.1 Name 
The name of this organization shall be **Speedcubing Bangladesh**.

## 1.2 Purpose
The purpose is to empower the speedcubing community of Bangladesh, help organize both official and unofficial Rubik’s Cube Competitions in Bangladesh, and maintain the speedcubing equipment of the cubing community of Bangladesh. 

## 1.3 values
Our values are community, respect, fairness, excellence, fun and volunteerism. 

# 2 Membership
## 2.1 Definition
A member of Speedcubing Bangladesh is a person who is associated with Speedcubing Bangladesh and is recognized by the Board of Directors.

## 2.2 Eligibility of Membership
Membership in the organization shall be open to any person. All memberships shall be granted upon a recommendation of a member and a majority vote of the board, or a majority vote of members at a properly announced meeting. Membership is nontransferrable.

## 2.3 Types of Membership
Speedcubing Bangladesh offers the following types of membership: 

1. Voting Members (Individual)
2. Non-voting Members (Individual)

The Board reserves the right to establish or remove membership categories.

### 2.3.1 Voting Members: 
The President, Vice-president, Executive Director(s), and Treasurer are voting members of the board. The membership is active from the date of their appointment, and is continued until their resignation/termination. 

### 2.3.2 Non-voting Members: 
The Advisor(s) are the non voting members. 

## 2.4 Annual Dues
The annual dues for each member shall be 0 Bangladeshi Taka(BDT).

## 2.5 Resignation and Termination
Any member may resign by filing a written resignation to the Board. Resignation shall not
relieve a member of unpaid dues, or other charges previously accrued. A member can have their membership terminated by a majority vote of the voting members. 

## 2.6 Exclusionary Clauses
The Board may deny or revoke membership or participation to any individual or group whose conduct is deemed harmful, unethical, or contrary to the organization’s values. Grounds for exclusion may include misconduct, conflict of interest, or actions detrimental to the cubing community.
Any exclusion shall require a majority vote of the Board and must be recorded in the official minutes.

# 3 Organizational Structure
The organizational hierarchy shall be as follows:

 - President 
 - Vice-president
 - Executive Director(s)
 - Treasurer
 - Advisors (Non-voting)
 - General Member(s) (Non-voting, subject to further review and approval by the Board of Directors)


# 4 Board of Directors
## 4.1 Powers 
The activities and affairs of Speedcubing Bangladesh shall be conducted, and all powers exercised, by or under the direction of the Board of Directors (the “Board”), subject to these Bylaws and applicable law.

## 4.2 Number and Compositions 
The Board shall consist of not less than four (4) and not more than fifteen (15) voting members, including the officers listed above.

## 4.3 Qualification of Directors
All directors must be individuals of good standing who have demonstrated integrity and interest in promoting speedcubing in Bangladesh. To ensure transparency and neutrality, no more than 49% of directors may be related by blood or marriage.

## 4.4 Terms of Office 
Each director shall serve a two-year term, renewable upon re-election. A director may serve a maximum of two consecutive terms, after which a one-year gap is required before reappointment.

## 4.5 Advisors 
Advisors may be appointed by the Board to provide guidance and mentorship. Advisors shall not have voting rights.

## 4.6 Voting and Decision-Making 
Only the voting members of the Board are eligible to vote on decision making scenarios. Vote has to be cast within the set time frame. Decisions shall be made by majority vote unless otherwise specified. No proxy voting shall be allowed. 

# 5 Meetings and Communication 
## 5.1 Regular Meetings
Regular Board meetings shall be held at times determined by the Board. At least one annual general meeting shall be conducted each year.

## 5.2 Quorum 
The members present at any properly announced meeting shall constitute a quorum if they are at least 1/3 of the total number of voting members.

## 5.3 Action Without a Meeting 
Decisions may be made electronically (via Discord or email) by a supermajority (2/3) vote of the Board.

# 6 Officers and Duties
## 6.1 President
The President shall preside at meetings, represent the organization to the WCA and external entities, and oversee execution of the organization’s objectives.

## 6.2 Vice President 
The Vice-President shall assist the President and act on their behalf in their absence.

## 6.3 Executive Director(s)
The Executive Director(s) shall manage daily operations, communications, records, and event coordination.

## 6.4 Treasurer 
The Treasurer shall manage the finances, maintain accurate accounts, and prepare annual financial statements for review by the Board.

## 6.5 Advisor(s)
Advisor(s) shall serve as non-voting consultants who provide counsel, expertise, and strategic direction to support the organization in upholding its values and fulfilling its stated purpose.

## 6.6 General Member(s)
General Member shall directly or indirectly help organize speedcubing competitions. 

# 7 Committees
The Board may form committees (Finance, Events, Outreach, etc.) as needed, chaired by directors or general members. Committees shall operate under Board supervision and have no independent decision-making power.

# 8 Inclusion of Other Organizations
To promote unity in the Bangladeshi cubing community:

- Speedcubing Bangladesh shall collaborate with other  NGOs, school/college/university clubs & organizations operating in Bangladesh.
- Such organizations may be formally accommodated under Speedcubing Bangladesh through partnership agreements or affiliate recognition, as determined by the Board.

# 9 Indemnification and Insurance
To the extent permitted by law, Speedcubing Bangladesh may indemnify its directors against legal expenses and liabilities incurred in good faith. The organization may also maintain liability insurance for its officers and directors.

# 10 Records and Reporting

- All official records shall be maintained by the Executive Director(s) and Treasurer.
- Annual reports summarizing activities and finances shall be reviewed by the Board and shared with the World Cube Association.
- The fiscal year shall be from July 1 to June 30.

# 11 Finances and Sponsorships
- The organization may accept donations, sponsorships, or grants consistent with its non-profit goals.
- Contracts or agreements must be signed by the President, Treasurer, or Executive Director, as authorized by the Board.
- No income shall personally benefit any director or member except as reimbursement for legitimate expenses.

# 12 Emergency Provisions
In emergencies, the Board may take necessary measures to ensure organizational continuity, provided all such decisions are documented and later ratified.

# 13 Office 
The principal office of Speedcubing Bangladesh for the transaction of its business shall be located at: 

``C-19, W1, Eastern Housing, Pallabi 2nd Phase, Dhaka-1216``

or in such other place as may be specified by resolution of the Board.

# 14 Amendments
These Bylaws may be amended or repealed by a supermajority (2/3) vote of the Board, provided that the proposed amendments are circulated at least seven days in advance.
All amendments shall be reported to the World Cube Association within a reasonable time.
